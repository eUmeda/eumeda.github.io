An Homage to Steven Strogatz

This site copies, as closely as it can, the design of mathematician Steven Strogatz's personal site, stevenstrogatz.com. There is a story about the novelist Kinoko Nasu, who loved Kodansha BOX so much that he published a doujinshi (a self-made book) that completely copied its design. I have done the same thing. The Japanese translations of Strogatz's books inspired me greatly in my undergraduate years. I admire him.

For general readers

Steven Strogatz (Japanese edition: supervised by Yoshiki Kuramoto, translated by Tsutomu Nagao) SYNC: The Emerging Science of Spontaneous Order Japanese edition: Hayakawa Bunko NF 403, the “Enjoying Mathematics” series — Hayakawa Publishing

A single volume brimming with the delight of using mathematics to unravel the phenomena of life, and with a researcher's stance toward the world. The Japanese edition is still on sale for Kindle.

Textbook

Steven H. Strogatz Nonlinear Dynamics and Chaos With Applications to Physics, Biology, Chemistry, and Engineering — 3rd ed., CRC Press (2024)

The standard textbook on nonlinear dynamics and chaos. A classic that conveys bifurcations, attractors, and chaos through equations, figures, and intuition.
