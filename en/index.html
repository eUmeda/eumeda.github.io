Eisaku Umeda (梅田栄作) — Mathematical Biology, Evolutionary Ecology, Plant Ecology

Solving the evolution of life through mathematics. 生命の進化を数理で解く

Eisaku Umeda studies the interplay between population dynamics and evolution. His fields are mathematical biology, evolutionary ecology, and plant ecology. He belongs to the Mathematical and Computational Biology Laboratory, Graduate School of Science, Tokyo Metropolitan University.
